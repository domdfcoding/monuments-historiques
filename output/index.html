Monuments Historiques

This list presents buildings and buildings that have been or are protected under Historic Monuments (classified or inscribed), from the year 1840 to the present. The list presents all the information available on the Mérimée base, which itself comes from the order and the protection file, drawn up by the Historic Monuments service. Protected buildings date from prehistory in the 20th century.
